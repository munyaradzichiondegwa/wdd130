Harare, Zimbabawe

Harare is one of the two Metropolitan provinces in Zimbabwe along with Bulawayo. It is also the Capital City of Zimbabwe and is bordered by the three Mashonaland Provinces, East, West and Central to its north. It is a metropolis composed of Harare proper, Chitungwiza as well as Ruwa and parts of Goromonzi. Harare is basically the nerve centre of Zimbabwe with most of the main functions of government, education, judiciary, research, transportation, industry and commerce centred around it. It is the city of government with the New Parliament in Mt Hampden having been recently completed
