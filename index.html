Zimbabwe

Zimbabwe is a landlocked country located in southeastern Africa, known for its diverse wildlife, Victoria Falls, and the historic Great Zimbabwe, an ancient stone city that was once the capital of the Kingdom of Zimbabwe. The country is home to multiple ethnic groups and has a rich cultural heritage marked by architectural marvels like the Great Zimbabwe ruins, which showcase the advanced civilization that existed between the 11th and 15th centuries.
