Rafting Adventure

Experience the thrill of navigating through some of the most exciting rivers! Our rafting adventures are designed for all levels, from beginners to expert thrill-seekers. Join us as we explore breathtaking landscapes, tackle challenging rapids, and make unforgettable memories along the way.

At Tugwi, our mission is to deliver unparalleled adventure experiences that connect people with nature while ensuring safety and fun.

History

Tugwi White Water Rafting was founded in 2010, stemming from a shared passion for the wild rivers and the thrill of adventure. Over the years, we have grown into a leading provider of rafting experiences, guiding thousands through our breathtaking local waters.
